Frequently Asked Questions:

If I purchased the Breakfast Package, where is breakfast served?

Upon arrival, our lovely receptionist will provide a pamphlet listing all breakfast locations with map markings and details.

How will I connect to the Wi-Fi in my room?

The password will be given to you upon check-in.

At what time is check-in?

Generally hotel rooms are available by 3 P.M., but guests may check-in before 3 P.M. if more convenient.

At what time is check-out?

Check-out is generally at 11 A.M. After check-out, the room will be cleaned for the subsequent guests.

Do you offer handicapped rooms?

Yes, each Modern Getaway location possesses designated handicapped rooms with appropriate accessible equipment.

Is parking available at the hotel?

All Modern Getaways have parking spaces allocated to each room.

What are the receptionâ€™s hours of operation?

The reception is open 24 hours and our colleagues will be at your service day and night.

Can I bring my pet?

At almost every Modern Getaway location, pets are welcome. Some Modern Getaway hotels will have a $10 fee when you check in.

Are the rates per person or per room?

All prices, unless stated otherwise, are on a per-room-basis.

Do you offer hotel shuttles, for example: to the next airport or train station?

Unfortunately, most hotels do not offer a hotel shuttle. Our colleagues will be happy to assist you in finding public transport or will call a taxi for you.

Can I have breakfast/dinner even if I am not staying in your hotel?

Absolutely; you can enjoy a meal or drink in one of our restaurants or bars.

What should I do if I lost or forgot something in my room?

Please contact the hotel reception. Our rooms are cleaned daily, so your lost item may have been found by our housekeeping team.

Which languages are spoken at reception?

Italian, English, French, German and Spanish.

Can I leave my luggage with reception upon check-in?

Yes, we can hold your luggage if your room is not available upon check-in. Additionally, we can hold your luggage upon check-out. We will store your luggage free of charge only on your check-in and check-out days.

Is room service available? If so, at what times?

Room service is available from 7am to 9pm everyday.

Do the bathrooms have baths or showers?

The bathrooms have shower with the exception of suite, which have a bath as well as a shower.
