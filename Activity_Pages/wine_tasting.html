Wine Tasting

One of our trained wine stewards will take you on a journey of the palate tempting your tastebuds with a selection of the very best wines from our cellar. You’ll even learn about what distinguishes red, white, and rosé wines apart from their color! Our wine steward will also be able to help you to discover how to tell the difference between different types of grapes, their taste, aroma and color, as well as the difference that certain types of barrels can have when used for aging!


Available Locations: Dallas, New York, San Francisco, Los Angeles
