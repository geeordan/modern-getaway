Snorkeling

Those who are unsure of how to use the snorkelling equipment correctly can ask an instructor to give them a basic introduction. You can see Manta Rays, Eel, Angel fish and even one or two Marlin if you’re lucky! This activity is the best way to meet other like-minded guests and discover the safest places to go snorkeling yourself. You can make use of the resort’s snorkelling equipment for free during your stay.


Available Locations: Honolulu, Los Angeles
